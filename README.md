TCIndexBundle
=============

Three entitites at the root of everything: 
### Category: a category of items, projects or articles or author presentation e.g.
### Item: an item, a projet or article e.g. 

The resulting page will look like this: 

    <template>
	<category 1>
		<text>
	<category 2>
		<text>
		<item 1>
		<item 2>
		<category 3>
			<text>
			<item 1>
			<item 2>
			<item 3>
			<item 4>
	<category 3>
		<item 1>
		<item 2>
	<template>
